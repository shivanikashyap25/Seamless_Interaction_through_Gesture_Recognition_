How it Recognizes Hand Gestures

1.Hand Landmark Detection: Mediapipe's hand tracking model is used to detect and track the position of hand landmarks in real-time.

2.Gesture Recognition: Specific gestures are recognized based on the relative positions of hand landmarks. For example, the distance between certain points is used to determine if a finger is raised or if a gesture corresponds to a drawing action.

1. Black

2. White

4. Blue

Shapes and Drawing Tools:

The application allows users to draw various shapes and freeform lines. The available tools are:

1.Freeform Draw: Allows for drawing freehand lines.

2.Line: Draws straight lines between two points.

3.Rectangle: Draws rectangles by selecting two diagonal corners.

4.Circle: Draws circles by defining the center and a point on the circumference.

5.Ellipse: Draws ellipses by selecting the center and defining the radii.

6.Triangle: Draws triangles by selecting three vertices.

7.Polygon: Draws polygons by selecting vertices.

8.Star: Draws stars by defining the center and outer radius.

9.Eraser: Erases parts of the drawing.

Color and Tool Selection:

The left side of the screen displays a vertical color panel where users can select colors by hovering over them. The right side of the screen shows a tool panel for selecting different shapes and drawing tools. Users can choose tools by hovering over the corresponding icons.
