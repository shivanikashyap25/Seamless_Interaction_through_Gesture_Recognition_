Steps to Use the Virtual Canvas Application

1.Launch the Application:

Run the provided script to start the webcam and initialize the drawing interface.

2.Color Selection:

Move your index finger to the color panel on the left side of the screen and select a color by pointing to it.

3.Tool Selection:

Move your index finger to the tools panel on the right side of the screen and select a tool by pointing to it.

4.Drawing:

Use the selected tool to draw on the screen by moving your hand within the webcam's view. Different gestures trigger different drawing actions: o Draw: Draw freeform lines by moving your finger. o Line, Rectangle, Circle, Ellipse, Triangle, Polygon, Star: Draw shapes by selecting the corresponding tool and performing gestures. o Eraser: Erase parts of the drawing by selecting the eraser tool and moving your finger over the areas to erase.

5.Undo/Redo:

Press 'Z' to undo the last action, and 'Y' to redo the undone action.

6.Save Drawing:

Press 'S' to save the current drawing as "painted_image.png" with a white background.

7.Screen Shot:

Press 'Ctrl+S' to save the Screen Shot.

8.Exit Application:

Press 'Q' or the ESC key to exit the application.
